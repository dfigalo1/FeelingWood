About This Wood
It is the national tree of Japan. It is also known as Empress Tree or Fenix (because once it is cut down, it grows up again faster than before). It was traditionally planted when a child was born and then when he got married. Later, its wood was used to build musical instruments, clogs or furniture that would belong to the new family.

Finish
In our "Colors" collection, our high-quality UV finish gives wonderful shades to the wood, without covering its grain and natural essence, giving a smooth finish that makes cleaning easy and increases wood protection.
