1. Easy To Install

Its reduced thickness makes cutting it very easy, the double-sided tape on the back simplifies the installation task, you just have to peel and stick

2. Easy Maitenance

All our woods are protected by a high quality UV finish, which favors easy cleaning with a dry or barely wet towel.

3. Sustainable

All our woods comes from implanted forests and forests with sustainable management. Our company uses practices to reduce electricity consumption and Co2 emissions.

4. Everlasting

Covering more than 42% of the rear surface of the boards, our high-quality double-sided tape exceeds the weight requirement of the wood by 4 times, achieving great strength and durability.

5. Double UV coating

All the boards have a UV finish on both sides, which provides greater stability against changes in temperature and humidity in the environment.

6. The right thickness

Our high-tech machinery allows us to cut the wood with high precision, offering the right thickness that provides solidity to the touch while maintaining the flexibility required to ensure the stability of the wall covering.
