How to Install

DIY Easy Peel and Stick Wall Paneling

To start the easy-peasy installation, you will need:

1) Cutter or electric/manual saw* for cutting 2) Ruler and square 3) Level 4) J-Roller 5) Gloves 6) Goggles (if you use a saw


*Pay attention to the hardness of the chosen wood to know which tool to choose.


Check! How many planks should I order?

First, choose the wood you would like to order for your surface. Measure the area on which you want to instal your planks. If you are measuring in feet, multiple the length times the width. If you are measuring in inches, multiple the length times the width and divide the number by 144. Calculate 5% more for possible wastes. Order your planks at www.feelingwood.com


Groovy! Feeling Wood arrived home! Now what?

1) Remove the packaging and leave the planks acclimatizing for at least 72 hours in the room you are going to install them in. 2) Start the installation from the middle area of the surface. 3) Use a level to draw a reference line where you will place your first piece. 4) Use a cutter or saw to cut the planks, remove the liner from each one and stick it over the surface using the reference line. 5) Pass a j-roller to make pressure.


Once you finish with all the planks, take a step away from the surface and contemplate your work! Feeling Good?
