Grapia

Grapia wood, also known as garapa, is a hardwood of tropical origin and interesting shades of gold.Grapia wood is mainly found in flooded areas of the Amazon rainforest of southern Brazil.

About This Wood
Guayubira is one of the woods with more personality in the world. It is also one of the most desired. This wood comes from South America, more specifically from Paraguay (Northwest of Argentina and South of Brazil). What makes this wood unique is that it has two opposite colours in one same board: a very dark brown color on one side and a sandy color on the other. This combination, divided in a cleary way, gives the possibility to make striking combinations.
