ABOUT US

Our history


We are hard core working people with more than four decades of experience in the industry. We grew up looking at how cabinetmakers by trade shaped wood as if it were a work of art, building eternal wooden furniture and decorative pieces that are still today a living testimony of the past.

Inspired by their craft and work (moulding the raw materials, creating a design, crafting the piece), we developed our very own recipe to keep the trade alive with a contemporary twist: we combine high technology and innovations with artisanal work and craftsmanship, through a careful and delicate worldwide wood selection process.

They all come from implanted forests or have been removed by sustainable forest maintenance procedures (considering energy optimization and the best utilization of the raw materials).
