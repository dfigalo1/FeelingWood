FAQ


Are the planks made of synthetic wood or other imitations?

No! We use 100% authentic and exotic woods from all over the world. You will notice once you get the sample box: the smell, the texture and the color don’t lie!


Do the planks have UV luster?

Yes! From both sides: it’s almost imperceptible but brings great protection. This is essential to ensure the durability of the wood

My wall it’s not in great shape. Anything to keep in mind?

Clean the surface with a damp towel to remove any dust or particles. Before you start, the surface must be dry! On a small and discreet area, stick a plank with pressure to ensure a good grip. After 48 hours, verify that the paint or surface texture has generated a correct adhesion.

Is FEELING WOOD a DIY product?

bsolutely! Just follow the steps at our installation section. If you have any doubts or need online help, just call us!

Let’s suppose I get bored of the planks… can I remove them?

It’s almost impossible to get bored of FEELING WOOD! But yes, you can remove the planks. It could take some hard work (it works like a wallpaper) and after that you may need some repairs and painting.

How much do the planks last?

Our premium adhesive of double-sided tape is rated for 10 years, but could last even more!
